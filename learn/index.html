About This Course

Most Claude education falls into two buckets: "here's how to sign up" beginner content, or deep developer tutorials. The critical middle layer — how Claude actually processes information and why configuration choices matter — doesn't exist as a structured learning path.

This course fills that gap. Each lesson is interactive, self-contained, and designed to build a mental model you can actually use when working with AI. No coding required.
